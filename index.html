Intro

Hello, I'm Felix Okpara, a data analyst passionate about discovering insights from complex data. I specialize in data cleaning, exploration, wrangling, visualization, and reporting, and I use a variety of tools, such as Excel, Power BI, Tableau, SQL, and Python to turn data into meaningful insights. Although I don't have any formal experience working on organizational projects, I've worked on several personal projects that have helped me develop my skills and refine my approach to data analysis.


Through these projects, I've learned the importance of a structured and methodical approach to data analysis and the value of creative problem-solving when dealing with complex datasets. My passion for data analysis goes beyond the technical aspects of the field. I see it as a way of telling stories and uncovering insights that can help individuals and organizations make better decisions.


I'm always looking for ways to improve my skills and stay up-to-date with the latest developments in data science. In my free time, I enjoy exploring new personal projects, learning about new technologies and business trends, and reading up on data science trends and best practices. I'm excited to continue growing my skills and exploring new opportunities to use data to make an impact.

Open Project



Python Project on
movie corellation

By utilizing Python libraries such as Pandas, NumPy, and Matplotlib, I conducted statistical analyses and generated visual representations that enabled me to make inferences about the connections between various movie characteristics.

One of the most noteworthy discoveries from my investigation was the significant positive correlation between budget and gross revenue. In simpler terms, this implies that movies with larger budgets generally have higher box office earnings.

This discovery has far-reaching implications for the movie industry as it suggests that investing more in the production of a movie could yield a greater financial reward. Additionally, by exploring other correlations between movie attributes, such as the relationship between ratings and genre, I was able to gain further insights into what factors drive the success of a movie.
